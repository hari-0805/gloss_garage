Replace the video below with your own footage.

----------------------------------------------
HERO VIDEO (plays in background of homepage)
----------------------------------------------

  hero-bg.mp4     → Background video for the hero section
                    Recommended: 1920×1080 or 1280×720
                    Format: MP4 (H.264 codec)
                    Duration: 10–30 seconds (loops automatically)
                    Size: Keep under 15MB for fast loading

----------------------------------------------
TIPS FOR YOUR VIDEO
----------------------------------------------
  - Shoot your team doing a real car wash at a customer's home
  - Good shots: foam being applied, wiping the car, clean final result
  - Avoid shaky footage — slow, steady panning looks best
  - Compress your video at handbrake.fr before replacing
  - The video plays muted (required by browsers for autoplay)
  - A dark overlay is applied automatically so text stays readable

----------------------------------------------
CURRENT FALLBACK
----------------------------------------------
  If hero-bg.mp4 is not found, the site uses a Pexels video URL.
  Once you add your own video file, it will use that instead.
